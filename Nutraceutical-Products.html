Calcium is a mineral used for animal’s body need to build and maintain strong bones to carry out important function. Calcium is the most and complementary mineral in the body of animals.

Calcitonic is essential supplement of calcium and vitamin A, D, E and B1 and play pivotal role. against metabolic and reproductive diseases. This happens when animals are in negative energy balance and stress and before and after parturition. So overcome this problem, calcitonic provides instant source of energy through calcium and vitamins.

INDICATIONS

  • Prevention and treatment of deficiency of calcium and vitamins A, D, E, and B1.
